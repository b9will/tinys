Our Story

Where neighbours become friends

Tiny's General Store began with a simple belief: that a neighbourhood deserves a place where quality meets warmth. Tucked away on Barton Ave, we've become part of the community's daily rhythm.

Every morning brings familiar faces and new discoveries. Whether it's your usual order or something that catches your eye on our shelves, there's always a reason to linger.
